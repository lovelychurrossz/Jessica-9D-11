Paris

Nawamo Dessert and Drinks is a historic café brand first established in 1780, with its original branch in the heart of Paris. Specializing in exquisite desserts and refreshing beverages, Nawamo is renowned for its unique topping — nata de watermelon — crafted from premium Densuke watermelons imported directly from Japan. Known for their luxurious sweetness and rarity, these Densuke watermelons bring an extraordinary flavor to every dish and drink. Every item at Nawamo Dessert and Drinks is carefully made to guarantee a delightful taste experience, with the nata de watermelon topping enhancing the freshness and quality of each creation. In addition to its roots in Paris, Nawamo proudly opened its first Indonesian branch in the vibrant city of Yogyakarta, offering locals and visitors a taste of authentic luxury desserts and drinks like no other. At Nawamo, every bite and sip is a celebration of tradition, innovation, and the finest ingredients from around the world.
